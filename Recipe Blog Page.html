Delicious Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients: milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until well combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased and floured cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool before serving.

Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chopped vegetables.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
  5. Serve immediately.
